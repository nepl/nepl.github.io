About Us

 

Continuing growth and diversification for bottom line results with risks well contained.

A group of people decided to extend their services with zeal and commitment for good workmanship in the field of Electricity, so the firm M/s Navdurga Electroconstruction Pvt. was established in the year 2000. The status of the firm was changed from Partnership to Private Limited Company in the year 2006.

Navdurga electroconstruction is a private limited company in the power sector with more than a decade of experience in the construction of transmission lines and sub stations. It has partnered with different power distribution companies for over 10 years and is currently an erection associate of many business houses involved in rural electrification, transmission lines, substations and other infrastructure construction for the government program as well as private infrastructure companies.

The knowledge and the experience of the firm are not stagnant but being kept closed to the modern and fast developing technology in the field. A close association with the workers in their typical problems, maintaining a good literature and technical books library, attending training programs, symposiums, seeking a close contact with houses have proved to be asset in the endeavour.

Our vision: We wish to be one of the leading players in the Power Sector with sustained growth & technological development at every step. At the same time we wish to give more importance to the nature and development of conventional source of energy, saving Water, re-charging ground water & optimum usage of Electricity.
